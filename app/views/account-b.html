Use NHS App services

Log in to view your health information and manage your healthcare online.

Access NHS App services using the NHS App or by logging in through the NHS website. You can create an account if you don't have one.

You must be aged 13 or over and registered at a GP surgery in England.

Continue

You can:

  • request repeat prescriptions
  • check test results
  • view messages from the NHS
  • book and manage appointments

Other ways to use NHS App services

You can also download the NHS App on your smartphone or tablet.
