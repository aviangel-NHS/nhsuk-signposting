How to book, change or cancel an appointment

If you need to book or change an appointment at your GP surgery, you may be able to:

  • use the appointment form on your GP surgery's website
  • log into the NHS App or the NHS website - you can create an account if you do not have one
  • contact the surgery by phone
  • go to the surgery and ask the receptionist

Before booking an appointment, your GP surgery may ask what you need help with.
